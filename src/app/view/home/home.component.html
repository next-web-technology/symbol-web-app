Wallet Hint

  • This wallet does not provide the feature to generate a new account.
  • You need to import an existing account that has a balance.
  • When importing, you need to specify the wallet name, address, private key, and password.
  • The private key, encrypted with the specified password, is stored in an IndexedDB that is readable only on your device's browser.
  • No information about the private key or password is stored on the server side.
  • In general, the IndexedDB will disappear when you close your browser or reboot your device. Please do not lose your private key and password information outside of this application.
